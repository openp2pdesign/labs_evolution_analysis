# labs_evolution_analysis
Jupyter Notebooks for analysing the historical evolution of Maker Laboratories (data from WHOIS, Twitter/X, Google Trends).

The research behind this software has taken place at the Politecnico di Milano during Massimo Menichinelli's stay as Senior Resident Researcher from 01/02/2021 to 08/01/2022. Further research was concluded afterwards at Elisava, Barcelona School of Design and Engineering (UVic-UCC).
